The Artist

A painter who plays by ear.

"I chase the same thing in paint that I chase on the fretboard — a mood you feel before you can name it."

Youmna's work lives in black and white: expressive, textured, a little restless. Ink circles that never quite close. Rain that falls in graphite. Fields that hum one low, sustained note.

Each piece is painted by hand, signed, and ready to hang. If one speaks to you, add it to your order — she'll reach out personally on WhatsApp to arrange delivery, anywhere in Lebanon.

— Youmna
